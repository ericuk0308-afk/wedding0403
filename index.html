How to get to PARC88
M

By MTR & Bus

Take the MTR to University Station (Exit B). Then take KMB Bus 272K or Green Minibus 27A to Science Park.

B

By Bus

Routes available: 272K (University Stn), 272A (University Stn), 272S (Diamond Hill/Kwun Tong).

P

Parking Info

Guest parking is available at Phase 2 Car Park (Basement). Electric vehicle charging stations are located on B2 floor.
